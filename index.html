Airbus a380

Airbus a380 is one of the most expensive, popular and powerful aeroplane in the world. A380 has one of the most powerful jet engines in the world, GE90 with over 1,10,000 horsepower. A380 was declared to Singapore airlines on 15 October 2007 and entered service on 25 October 2007 with flight number SQ380 between Singapore and Sydney.
